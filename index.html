About King Site
A new, independently-run tool

King Site is a new platform built on Deriv's public trading API. It is not Deriv itself, and it's not an established brand with a long track record — it's a small, independently-run project.

King Site earns a small markup on trades placed through the platform via Deriv's official partner program. This is separate from your win or loss on any individual trade — you keep whatever your trading results are; we earn our markup either way.

Trading derivatives carries real risk. Automated strategies like Martingale can lose money quickly during a losing streak. No bot or strategy on this site guarantees a profit. Only trade with money you can afford to lose, and consider starting with a small stake while you get a feel for how a strategy behaves.
